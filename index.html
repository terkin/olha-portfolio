Case Study 01

Book a Demo
Form Redesign

UX Research Conversion Optimisation Lead Gen Form Design Figma
Industry
B2B SaaS · Fintech
Scope
Lead-gen form UX redesign
Year
2023

A B2B fintech platform's sales team was losing high-intent leads at the "Book a Demo" stage. A bloated, single-page form with no scheduling integration and zero lead-recovery logic meant hard-won paid traffic was converting at a fraction of its potential. This redesign stripped out every unnecessary step — turning a 7-minute ordeal into a <2-minute progressive flow that captured more leads and booked them faster.

The Challenge

Four pain points killing conversions

⚡
Form Fatigue
A single long page with 12+ required fields caused immediate abandonment. Users felt the effort outweighed the value.
📭
Communication Gaps
Only an email field was offered. No phone, LinkedIn, or alternative channel — causing leads to vanish when emails bounced.
🗓
Scheduling Delays
After submitting, users waited 48–72 hours for a manual callback. High-intent leads went cold before the first contact.
💸
Lost Opportunities
No partial-save or recovery mechanism. A session timeout meant all entered data was lost and rarely re-entered.

Design Solution

A 3-step progressive flow

Each step asks only what is needed at that moment — reducing perceived effort, increasing completion, and building trust incrementally.

Step 01
Contact Information
Minimal, progressive entry
  • Reduced to 4 essential fields: name, company, email, and role.
  • Inline validation provides instant feedback — no submit-and-regret.
  • Smart auto-complete for company name removes friction for known brands.

Thinking Behind the Design

Four principles, one coherent flow

01
Cognitive Load Reduction
Progressive disclosure breaks a 12-field wall into 3 focused micro-steps. Each screen asks only what is needed at that moment.
02
Flexible Communication
Offering phone, LinkedIn, and email as parallel channels means no lead disappears due to a single bounced address.
03
Accelerated Sales Cycle
Embedded scheduling removes the 48–72h manual callback lag. Leads book a slot while interest is highest.
04
Zero-Loss Lead Recovery
Partial-save and fallback capture ensure that even incomplete sessions generate a usable contact point.
